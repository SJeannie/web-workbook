Jeannie Kim - Curriculum Vitae

Ms. Jeannie with her students

Current Projects

Jeannie is working on WriteBack, a project-sharing platform where K-12 student users can share their writing to give and receive feedback on each other's work.

Related Experience

Jeannie is also the Lead Guide and Curriculum Designer at Talent Unbound, an Acton Academy in Houston. In lieu of direct instruction as teachers do, guides at Acton Academies provide curricula to engage students in project-based learning. In addition to curricula design, Jeannie confers with students on their individual writing and revising.

Prior to joining Talent Unbound, Jeannie worked at KIPP Sharpstown College Prep as the seventh grade Reading and Writing Teacher. Her students received the highest score on the Writing STAAR (Texas end-of-course exam) and the second-highest scores on the Reading STAAR within the KIPP Houston region for two consecutive years.

Education and Other Experience

Before finding her calling in education, Jeannie worked as an equity analyst at Dalton Advisors, equity long-short fund Dalton Investment's on-ground Shanghai office for its Greater China and Asia ex-Japan strategy funds.

Prior to being recruited to join Dalton, Jeannie worked in Duff & Phelp's LA office as a Valuation Analyst.

Jeannie is a graduate of the Huntsman International Studies and Business Program at the University of Pennsylvania. She was the Jamshed Ghandi Scholarship Recipient and has a Bachelor of Science in Economics from the Wharton School and a Bachelor of Arts in International Studies from the College of Arts and Sciences.
